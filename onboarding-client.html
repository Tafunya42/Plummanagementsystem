Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an artist?
Browse our artist directory, click on the artist you're interested in, review their profile and rates, then click "Check Availability" or "Book Now" to proceed with your booking.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, you can cancel with decreased penalty depending on when you cancel. Cancellations 7+ days before the event receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may have a penalty.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major payment methods including mobile money (MTN, Airtel), bank transfers, and online payments. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
Are the prices in Zambian Kwacha?
Artist rates are displayed in Zambian Kwacha (K). Plum charges no hidden fees - what you see is what you pay (plus the 15% platform fee which is shown before booking).
What if an artist cancels?
If an artist cancels, you'll receive a full refund immediately. We can also help you find an alternative artist for your event.
How do I contact support?
You can reach our support team via email at support@plum.com, through the contact form in your dashboard, or by calling our support line. We're available 24/7 for urgent issues.
