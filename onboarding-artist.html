Tips for Success

High-Quality Profile Photo
Use a clear, professional profile photo. High-quality images attract more bookings and help clients remember you.
Detailed Descriptions
Write compelling descriptions of your experience, specialties, and what makes you unique. Help clients understand what you offer.
Portfolio & Media
Add photos and videos of your performances, past events, or highlights. Social proof significantly increases bookings.
Respond Quickly
Reply to booking inquiries within 24 hours. Fast responses increase your chances of getting booked.
Competitive Pricing
Research market rates in Zambia and set prices that reflect your experience and value. You can adjust over time.
Ask for Reviews
After completing bookings, ask satisfied clients to leave reviews. Positive reviews build trust and attract more business.

Important Information

15% Platform Fee

Plum charges a 15% platform fee on all bookings. You receive 85% of the booking amount after the event is completed.

Payment Policy
