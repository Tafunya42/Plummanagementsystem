Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2025 | Last Updated: March 13, 2026

Plum Entertainment ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our Platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, phone number, date of birth
  • Identity Documents: ID numbers, ID scans, passport information
  • Address Information: Residential address, city, province, postal code
  • Financial Information: Bank account details, payment method information
  • Professional Information: Experience, credentials, portfolio, rates
  • Profile Content: Photos, videos, descriptions, reviews

1.2 Usage Information

  • Browsing history and activity on the Platform
  • Device information and IP address
  • Communication records with other users
  • Payment and booking history

1.3 Cookies & Tracking

  • We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience
  • Analytics tools help us understand user behavior
  • You can control cookie settings in your browser

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Account creation and management
  • Processing bookings and payments
  • User verification and fraud prevention
  • Customer support and communications
  • Platform improvement and research
  • Legal compliance and regulatory requirements
  • Marketing communications (with your consent)

3. Data Protection & Security

Your personal information is protected using industry-standard encryption and security measures.

3.1 Security Measures

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive financial data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Restricted access to personal information
  • Employee confidentiality agreements

3.2 Your Responsibilities

  • Protect your account login credentials
  • Do not share your password with others
  • Notify us immediately of unauthorized access

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

4.1 When We Share Information

  • With Other Users: Limited profile information is visible to facilitate bookings
  • Payment Processors: Financial information is shared with secure payment gateways
  • Legal Compliance: Information may be disclosed to comply with law enforcement requests
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors may process data on our behalf (all bound by confidentiality)

4.2 Data NOT Shared

  • Your bank account details are never shared with other users
  • Personal identification documents are never sold to third parties
  • Your contact information is not used for marketing without consent

5. Data Retention

  • Active Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data retained for 2 years after account deletion
  • Financial Records: Retained for 7 years for tax compliance
  • Legal Requirements: Data may be retained if required by law

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access & Portability

  • You can request a copy of your personal data
  • You can export your data in a portable format
  • Requests are processed within 30 days

6.2 Right to Correction

  • You can update or correct your personal information
  • Use your account settings or contact support

6.3 Right to Deletion

  • You can request account deletion and data removal
  • Some data may be retained for legal/compliance reasons
  • Processing time: 30 days

6.4 Right to Opt-Out

  • You can opt out of marketing communications
  • Preferences can be managed in your account settings
  • We respect "Do Not Track" signals

7. Children's Privacy

  • Plum is not intended for users under 18 years old
  • We do not knowingly collect data from minors
  • If we learn a user is a minor, we will delete their information

8. International Data Transfers

  • Your data may be processed in Zambia or other jurisdictions
  • We maintain appropriate safeguards for international transfers
  • By using Plum, you consent to such transfers

9. Third-Party Links & Services

  • Plum may contain links to third-party websites
  • We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices
  • Review their privacy policies before sharing information

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

  • Plum may update this Policy at any time
  • Significant changes will be communicated via email
  • Continued use implies acceptance of changes
