Platform Code of Conduct

Effective Date: March 1, 2025 | Last Updated: March 13, 2026

Welcome to Plum! This Code of Conduct outlines the professional standards and expectations for all artists using the Plum platform. By registering and using our services, you agree to abide by these guidelines.

1. Professional Conduct

1.1 Professionalism

  • Always conduct yourself in a professional manner during all interactions with clients and the Plum team
  • Treat all clients, event organizers, and fellow artists with respect and courtesy
  • Respond to inquiries and messages within 24 hours (during business hours)
  • Maintain a positive and collaborative attitude

1.2 Communication

  • Communicate clearly about services, pricing, and terms
  • Keep clients informed of any changes or important details
  • Use professional language in all communications
  • Do not engage in harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior

2. Service Delivery Standards

2.1 On-Time Performance

  • Arrive at least 15 minutes before the scheduled performance or event
  • Complete all services as agreed in the booking
  • Maintain high quality performance standards
  • If delayed, notify the client immediately
Repeated tardiness may result in account warnings or suspension.

2.2 Service Quality

  • Provide services that match your profile description
  • Bring all necessary equipment and materials
  • Ensure professional appearance and presentation
  • Comply with any specific client requirements (agreed in advance)

3. Legal & Compliance Requirements

3.1 Accurate Information

  • Provide truthful and accurate information on your profile
  • Update your information if any details change
  • Do not misrepresent your experience, qualifications, or credentials
  • Provide genuine and recent portfolio materials

3.2 Documentation Compliance

  • Maintain valid identification documents as required
  • Comply with all background check and verification requests
  • Provide accurate banking information for payments
  • Report any changes to legal/personal information promptly

4. Cancellation & Booking Policies

4.1 Cancellation Terms

  • 7+ days before event: Cancel with no penalty
  • 48 hours - 7 days before: 25% cancellation fee
  • 24-48 hours before: 50% cancellation fee
  • Less than 24 hours: 100% cancellation fee (no refund to client)
Important: Cancellations must be made through the Plum platform, not directly with the client. This protects both parties.

4.2 No-Show Policy

  • If you fail to show up for a booked event without prior notice:
  • The client receives a full refund
  • Your account receives a formal warning
  • Repeated no-shows will result in account suspension

5. Payment & Financial Integrity

5.1 Payment Processing

  • Plum retains 15% platform fee on all bookings
  • You receive 85% of the agreed booking amount
  • Payments are processed 48 hours after event completion
  • You can view payment details in your dashboard

5.2 Refund Disputes

  • Clients can request refunds for unmet service expectations
  • Refund requests are reviewed within 48 hours
  • Provide evidence of service completion if requested
  • Repeated disputes may trigger account review

6. Prohibited Conduct

The following behaviors are strictly prohibited and may result in immediate account suspension:

  • Discrimination: Refusing service based on race, gender, religion, or other protected characteristics
  • Harassment: Any form of harassment, bullying, or abusive behavior
  • Fraud: Misrepresenting services, qualifications, or submitting false documents
  • Off-Platform Transactions: Arranging payments outside the Plum platform to avoid fees
  • Safety Violations: Any behavior that endangers client safety or comfort
  • Illegal Activities: Any involvement in illegal activities at events or off-platform
  • Inappropriate Content: Sharing explicit, offensive, or inappropriate material

7. Content & Intellectual Property

7.1 Your Content

  • You retain ownership of your creative work and performances
  • You grant Plum permission to use your profile content for marketing
  • You must have rights to all content you upload
  • Do not upload copyrighted material without permission

8. Privacy & Confidentiality

  • Keep client information confidential
  • Do not share client details with third parties
  • Do not use client information for personal gain or marketing
  • Respect client privacy and disclose limitations upon request

9. Account Suspension & Termination

9.1 Grounds for Suspension

Plum may suspend your account for:

  • Repeated violations of this Code of Conduct
  • Fraudulent activity or document falsification
  • Safety concerns or criminal activity
  • Severe breach of client trust or service quality
  • Payment disputes or financial irregularities

9.2 Appeal Process

  • If suspended, you will receive notification with reasons
  • You have 14 days to appeal the suspension
  • Appeals are reviewed by our compliance team
  • The decision is final after review

10. Acknowledgment & Agreement

By registering as a Plum artist, you acknowledge that:

  • You have read and understood this Code of Conduct
  • You agree to comply with all outlined standards
  • You understand violations may result in account action
  • You accept Plum's right to update these guidelines
Note: This Code of Conduct applies to all artist activities on the Plum platform. Plum reserves the right to modify these guidelines with 30 days notice.
